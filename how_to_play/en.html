The Unic

Find & Double Tap on the Unic !

Rules are pretty simple :
Every Pattern will be represented Twice, in 2 distinct Shapes named Duplicates...
EXCEPT for ONE :
THE UNIC !
Find the Shape containing The Unic Pattern and Tap on IT!
Tap again to confirm
Tip: Simply Double-Tap on the Unic when you feel confident!
Each time you unveil a Unic, a Duplicate disappears, generating a brand new Unic.

Oops, that was not The Unic...

If by mistake you double-tap on a Duplicate instead of the Unic, it will start blinking...
To repair a blinking Shape, Double-Tap its Duplicate

Flip Duplicates

To regain some time, you can "flip" Duplicates.
Find two shapes containing the same pattern : Tap on the first one, then tap on the second one...
if they were true duplicates, both shapes will "flip" to wood and you’ll gain some time back !
Flips are the best way to win, BUT ... you’ll have a limited number per game, so use them well !
If you run out of Flip : Activate the "Bunch of Flip" Booster !

Boosters

There are several types of Boosters that will prove very helpful
Time Freeze
Hint
Unic Bomb
Bunch of Flip
They will appear at the bottom of the game board, and are activated by a single Tap



Zen Mode

No Timer, no stress...
Unlocked with 3 Packs in Deck

Crazy Flip Mode

Flip as much as you want !
Unlocked with 4 Packs in Deck
What's the Difference?!
An Endless Stock of Flips!
And also... a Terribly short Timer... unbeatable, unless you flip as many duplicates as you can inside the hint Area, until the Unic unveils.
As the final goal remains: find The Unic!
